Featured Project

Lead Role in UW-Madison’s 2025 Mainstage Production of Spring Awakening

This spring, I had the incredible opportunity to portray Melchior in Spring Awakening, UW-Madison’s 2025 mainstage musical. A powerful coming-of-age story set against the backdrop of a repressive society, this Tony Award-winning production challenged me both emotionally and artistically—and remains one of my most meaningful experiences on stage.

The show ran April 17–27, 2025 at the Mitchell Theatre in Madison, WI.
