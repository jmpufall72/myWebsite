About Me

I’m a student at the University of Wisconsin–Madison, majoring in Finance, Investment, and Banking with a Certificate in Theater and Drama. With a background that bridges business and the arts, I’m passionate about creating bold, story-driven experiences that connect with people in meaningful ways.

As an actor who thrives on creative problem-solving, I’m especially drawn to the intersection of entertainment, innovation, and technology. I enjoy crafting strategies that bring ideas to life—whether through developing new business approaches or exploring how emerging technologies can unlock new forms of storytelling.

Long-term, I aim to launch my own venture in the entertainment space—blending storytelling, strategy, and innovation to help shape the future of the industry and transform how stories are told and experienced.
