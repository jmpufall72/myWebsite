Overview of My Technical Skills

My work in technology is driven by a passion for problem-solving and innovation, always with a focus on creating practical, impactful solutions that enable inspiring storytelling. Whether I'm coding dynamic applications, developing websites, or building immersive 3D environments, my goal is to combine creativity with technical precision to craft systems that engage audiences and enhance the storytelling experience.

With a foundation in computer science and finance, I am deeply passionate about the transformative potential of AI and technology in the entertainment industry. I believe we are on the brink of a revolution where these advancements will reshape how stories are told, experienced, and connected with global audiences. I am determined to be at the forefront of this revolution, constantly learning and adapting to stay ahead. My work spans from web development to using tools like Unreal Engine to build 3D cinematic environments and SolidWorks for mechanical design, reflecting my commitment to mastering both creative and technical disciplines. I aim to contribute to this shift by blending technology, storytelling, and design to craft experiences that inspire and leave a lasting impact.

Web Development

This website serves as my personal portfolio, built from the ground up to showcase both my technical and artistic projects. The design focuses on user experience, simplicity, and showcasing my skills in web development, design, and content creation.

Personal Portfolio Website

The website is custom-built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with a focus on clean, responsive design. I utilized GitHub Pages for hosting and structured the layout to be both visually engaging and easy to navigate. Key features include:

  • A responsive layout that adapts to different screen sizes and devices.
  • Interactive sections for my technical and artistic portfolios.
  • Custom CSS stylesheets for different pages, improving page load speed and organization.
  • Professional sections such as an About Me page, Resume, and Future Plans.
  • Embedded project showcases, including links to my work in music, acting, and coding.

Building this portfolio has allowed me to integrate my skills in front-end development and design while keeping the user experience as a top priority.
